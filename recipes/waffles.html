Waffles recipe

Wake up on the right side of the bed with this top-rated waffle recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 Large eggs
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cups of milk
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 4 teaspoon salt or to taste
  • 1 cup butter melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Preparation

  1. Mix flour, salt, baking powder, and sugar together in a bowl; set aside.
  2. Beat eggs in a separate bowl, stir in milk, butter, and vanilla.
  3. Pour milk mixture into flour mixture; beat until blended.
  4. Ladle butter into a preheated waffle iron.
  5. Cook waffles until golden and crisp.
