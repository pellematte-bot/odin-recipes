Lasagna

Make the meat sauce. Cook the noodles. Make the ricotta mixture.

Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions. Cover with foil and bake. Let the lasagna rest before serving

  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato
  • Sugar
  • Spices
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Prepare the Dish

  1. Meat sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta Mixture
  4. Mozzarella slices
  5. Meat Sauce
  6. Parmesan Cheese
  7. Repeat the layers, then top with the remaining Parmesan
